<h1>Privacy Policy for Dips</h1>

<h2>Introduction</h2>


<p>

Dips ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that provides beach information,

including water temperatures, air temperatures, and swimming condition guidance.

</p>


<p>

This Privacy Policy explains what information Dips uses and how that information is handled.

</p>


<h2>Information We Use</h2>


<h3>Location Information</h3>


<p>

With your permission, Dips may access your device's location to identify beaches near you

and provide relevant beach conditions.

</p>


<p>

Location information is used only while the app is running and is not stored, collected,

or shared by Dips beyond what is necessary to provide location-based functionality.

</p>


<p>

You may disable location access at any time through your device settings.

</p>


<h3>Search Information</h3>


<p>

When you search for a location within Dips, the search query may be processed through

location and weather service providers in order to return relevant results and weather information.

</p>


<p>

Searches are not stored by Dips.

</p>


<h2>Third-Party Services</h2>


<p>

Dips uses third-party services to retrieve weather and environmental information.

</p>


<h3>OpenWeather</h3>


<p>

Weather information displayed within the app is provided by OpenWeather.

</p>


<p>

OpenWeather Privacy Policy:

<a href="https://openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy">

https://openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy

</a>

</p>

<p>

Dips does not require user registration or accounts.

</p>


<p>

Dips does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information for marketing purposes.

</p>


<p>

We do not maintain user profiles or databases containing personal information.

</p>


<h2>Analytics and Advertising</h2>


<p>

Dips does not use advertising networks, behavioral tracking, or third-party analytics services.

</p>
